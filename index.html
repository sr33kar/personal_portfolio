Multi-Objective Optimization for Autonomous Agents

Feb 2024 - Mar 2024

Implemented a multi-objective Markov Decision Process (MDP) with lexicographic reward preferences to address unintended negative side effects (NSE) in autonomous agents. The solution utilizes Lexicographic Value Iteration (LVI) for optimizing policies while integrating slack variables to balance primary and secondary objectives. Implemented in a Pygame environment, the approach is demonstrated across various grid sizes, enhancing decision-making in stochastic environments and advancing the safety and reliability of autonomous systems.
